About Me

My name is Glory Emmanuel Aviña and I work at Sandia National Laboratories in Livermore, California. I enjoy applying the scientific method to national security problems. My research focuses on cognitive science, enhancing human performance, and cyber security. I've worked at Sandia for 13 years - 10 years in Albuquerque, NM and 3 years in Livermore. My favorite hobby is international travel. I've been to 32 countries and 6 continents. I really enjoy learning about other cultures and different perspectives, as well as seeing how no matter where I am in the world, there are others who share similar joys and frustrations. I'm really passionate about fighting injustice at a global level and hope I will have more opportunities to pursue that.
